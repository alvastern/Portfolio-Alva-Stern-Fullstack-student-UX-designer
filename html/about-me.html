About me

Hi!

My name is Alva Stern, and I am a UX/UI designer currently studying full-stack development. I have a strong interest in the entire web development process and aim to work across all stages, from user research and needs analysis, to user-centred design, development of accessible, to fully implemented websites.

As a person, I am creative and solution-oriented, while also being structured and methodical in my way of working. In team settings, I enjoy taking responsibility for organising work and contributing new ideas and perspectives. I am driven, like to move work forward, and feel comfortable working both collaboratively and independently.

If my skills and experience sound relevant to what you or your organisation are looking for, or if you have any questions about me or my projects, feel free to contact me.
